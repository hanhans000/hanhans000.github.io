Synesthesia Garden

Welcome to the Synesthesia Garden!

In the game, you’re not just nurturing a plant

you’re engaging in an experience designed to highlight the delicate balance of care that plants

and by extension, all life—require

How to Play

With each stage, your goal is to nurture it mindfully by fulfilling three essential needs: Water, Shine Light, and Touch Plant.

Each button contributes to growth,
but be cautious,

overcaring like pressing too fast or repeating actions, can be harmful, and the plant may die.

Remember to explore the site

As you care for your plant, keep an eye out for extra features that add a bit of magic to the experience.
